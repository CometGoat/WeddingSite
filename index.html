Travel and Accommodation

Viaggio e Alloggio

Our wedding is taking part in the beautiful Euganean hills in the Veneto region of northern Italy. The closest major airport is Venice Marco Polo, with a further one hour by road to reach the area, or just 40 minutes to reach the city of Padova.

Padova, Giulia’s hometown, and birthplace of the Aperol Spritz, is the closest city to the hills, and offers many things to see if you wish to extend your stay. Also, Montegrotto Terme is a nearby spa town known for its many hotels. Both Padova and Montegrotto Terme are excellent places to stay for the wedding, and we will arrange bus transport to the venue from these areas.

We recommend looking for accommodation early, and are happy to help with finding any hotels or to answer any questions during your search.

We suggest that travel is made at least the day before the wedding, leaving early enough for ample time to travel to your hotel from the airport if using public transport. Taxi services are also available, and we are happy to help facilitate these.
